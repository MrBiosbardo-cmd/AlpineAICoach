The problem

A gap that has existed for too long

In most parts of the world, a cyclist who wants structured, science-backed training has two options. Pay for a professional coach they cannot afford, or train without guidance and simply hope for the best.

The tools that exist were built for markets that already have everything. They assume you have a power meter, a premium subscription, and a coach on standby.

Most cyclists in the world have none of that. They train on passion, on instinct, on whatever they can piece together. That is not a lack of dedication. It is a lack of access. And that gap is unfair.

Alpine AI Coach was built because that gap is not inevitable. The knowledge exists. The technology exists. The only thing missing was someone willing to close it.

The solution

Professional coaching intelligence. No power meter required.

Alpine AI Coach is a free coaching platform available on Telegram and Web App, giving every cyclist access to personalised, science-backed training analysis. Log your sessions, upload data, and receive intelligent feedback grounded in real sport science.

RPE Analysis

Rate of Perceived Exertion analysis that translates how your body actually felt into actionable training insight. Available free, always.

Heart Rate Analysis

Deeper physiological insight from your heart rate data. Understanding your cardiovascular response means training smarter, not just harder.

Personalised Feedback

Not generic advice. Responses built around your specific session, your perceived effort, and your individual context.

Works on Any Device

Use Alpine AI Coach in Telegram or in the Web App, whichever fits your routine. No expensive hardware, no complicated setup, and no barriers to getting structured coaching support.
